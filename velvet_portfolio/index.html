About

Crafting Visual Stories That Resonate

I'm a multidisciplinary designer and art director with over 8 years of experience creating bold, memorable visual identities for brands that dare to stand out.

My work sits at the intersection of art and commerce, blending brutalist aesthetics with refined elegance. I believe in the power of contrast—dark and light, chaos and order, tradition and rebellion.

Based in New York, I collaborate with forward-thinking clients worldwide, transforming abstract concepts into tangible visual experiences that captivate and inspire.
