Our Story

Sourcing the Finest Beans Since 2015

What started as a dream between two friends with a shared passion for exceptional coffee has grown into a beloved neighborhood institution.

We believe in direct trade relationships with farmers, roasting in small batches to highlight each bean's unique character, and creating a space where everyone feels at home.
